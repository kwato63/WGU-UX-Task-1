Welcome to Taniti

Taniti is a tropical paradise with sandy beaches, lush rainforests, and a vibrant local culture. With a population of about 20,000, the island offers a mix of traditional charm and modern tourist appeal.

History

Although small in size, Taniti has a rich history shaped by its indigenous people and its growing tourism industry. Once reliant on fishing and agriculture, the island is now becoming a popular vacation destination.
