What You Need to Know

  • Currency: US Dollar (some businesses accept euros and yen)
  • Power: 120 volts (US standard)
  • Transportation: Buses, taxis, rental cars, bikes
  • Emergency Services: One hospital, multilingual clinics
  • Language: English widely spoken in Taniti City
  • Crime: Low, but beware of pickpocketing in crowded areas
